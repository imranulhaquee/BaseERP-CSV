Accounting Software for Small and Medium Size Business


BaseFR is a finance and accounting software solution with VAT compliance capabilities that offers comprehensive services for businesses in UAE.


The accounting and finance system serves as the fundamental support structure for any business. BaseFR assist businesses in effectively managing their accounts, cash flow, and credit.


With baseFr, you can create invoices that comply with VAT regulations, generate financial reports, perform and manage tax calculations with timely and accurate data.


Manage Everything In One Place



Our software allows businesses to streamline all their financial activities in one place, including accounting, budgeting, forecasting, consolidation, dashboards, reporting, scenario planning. It provides an efficient and accurate way to manage finances, enabling businesses to make informed decisions and stay on top of their financial activities.
